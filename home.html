New Community Center Opening

We are excited to announce the opening of our new community center located at the heart of the neighborhood. The center will provide a variety of services including fitness classes, workshops, and community events. Grand opening date: 15th December 2024.

Volunteer Program Launch

We are launching a volunteer program to help clean and beautify local parks. If you're interested in giving back to your community, join us for the first clean-up day on 20th December 2024. Together, we can make our community a better place!
